Chorus: All my life I give to You, O Lord. All my life I give to You. I give to You as You gave to me, All my life I give to You.

You led us out of darkness when we knew not where to go; You asked us then, to follow You, and we said we would go.

You suffered for the sake of man, that we might live with You. Oh, let us show our thankfulness in all we say and do.
