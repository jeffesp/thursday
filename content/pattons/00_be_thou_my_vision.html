Be Thou my vision, O Lord of my heart; Be nothing else to me, but what You are. You're my best thought, by day or by night; Waking or sleeping, Your presence my light.

Be my true Wisdom, and be my true Word; I ever with You, and You with me Lord. You're my great Father, and I'm Your true son; You in me dwelling, and I with You one.

Riches I need not, nor man's empty praise; You're my inheritance, now and always. You and You only, first in my heart; Lord King of heaven, my treasure You are.

Lord King of heaven, my victory won; I can see heaven's joys, bright heaven's sun. Heart of my own heart, whatever befall; Still be my vision, O ruler of all.
