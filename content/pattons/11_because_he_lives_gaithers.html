God sent His Son, they called Him, Jesus; He came to love, heal, and forgive; He lived and died to buy my pardon, an empty grave is there to prove my Savior lives!

Chorus: Because He lives, I can face tomorrow, because He lives, all fear is gone; because I kno-o-ow He holds the future, and life is worth the living just because He lives!

How sweet to hold a newborn baby, and feel the pride and joy he gives; but greater still the calm assurance: this child can face uncertain days because He lives!

(repeat Chorus)

And then one day, I'll cross the river, I'll fight lifes' final war with pain; and then, as death gives way to vict'ry, I'll see the lights of glory and I'll know He lives!

(repeat Chorus)
